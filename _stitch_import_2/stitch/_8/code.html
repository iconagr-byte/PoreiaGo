General Inquiries

How do I change my flight reservation? expand_more
You can modify your flight dates or times directly through the "Bookings" tab in your profile. Select the specific trip and choose "Modify Flight." Please note that airline fees and fare differences may apply depending on your ticket class.
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if cancelled within 24 hours of booking, provided the travel date is at least 7 days away. Beyond that, refund eligibility depends on the specific rate type purchased.
Do I need travel insurance? expand_more
While not mandatory, we highly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment against unexpected medical emergencies, trip interruptions, or delays. You can add this during checkout.
How do I access my itinerary offline? expand_more
You can download a PDF copy of your full itinerary from the confirmation page or the "Bookings" section. Once downloaded, it will be available on your device without an internet connection.
